solar tunnel dryer

In subtropical countries, like Nigeria, a high percentage of crops spoils during the drying period due to permanently high relative humidity and sudden rainfalls. Open air sun drying and smoke drying, lead to insufficient product quality. Hence the need for a solution that satisfy not only medium and big commercial farmers, but also rural communities that lack power to process and preserve their products. Oriabure provides Solar Tunnel Dryer, which only input is the sun.
